About Me

As an Adjunct Lecturer at BCC, I teach Cybersecurity (CSN 150) and Ethical Hacking & Penetration Testing (CSN 160), covering topics from OSI/TCP-IP fundamentals and IoT hardware hacking to advanced pentesting methodologies.

Research & Projects

IEEE Access — Journal Paper (In Progress)

Cross-Dataset Generalization in Deepfake Detection: A Comparative Study of Neurosymbolic Multimodal and Single-Modality Approaches

Co-authored with Prof. Fatma Najar. Investigates how well deepfake detection models trained on one dataset generalize to others. Key finding: high in-distribution accuracy does not predict cross-dataset robustness. SuGuard drops from 98.25% to 48% cross-dataset; DeepGuard improves by 9.25 points cross-dataset. Physiology modality shows highest stability (153.2% retention).

Datasets: FakeAVCeleb v1.2, Celeb-DF-v2
Best Accuracy: 98.25%
Best AUC: 0.9993
Project — SuGuard (PRISM Symposium)

SuGuard: A 4-Modality Neurosymbolic Deepfake Detection Pipeline

A multimodal deepfake detection system combining Visual (EfficientNet-B0), Audio (MFCC heuristic), Text (Whisper + DistilRoBERTa), and Physiology (rPPG) modalities. Uses weighted fusion (visual 0.40, audio 0.25, text 0.25, physiology 0.10) with inter-modal consistency scoring. Deployed via Flask with a forensic reasoning panel and temporal spike chart.

Accuracy: 98.25%
F1 Score: 98.27%
AUC: 0.9993
Dataset: FakeAVCeleb v1.2
GitHub: suguard-multimodal-deepfake-detection
Project — DeepGuard (OSRC)

DeepGuard: Progressive Curriculum EfficientNet-B4 Deepfake Classifier

A single-modality visual deepfake classifier built on EfficientNet-B4 trained via progressive curriculum learning (50→100→200 videos/class) on Celeb-DF-v2. Deployed as a Flask web application. Demonstrates that single-modality models can achieve competitive accuracy with simpler architecture.

Accuracy: 98%
Dataset: Celeb-DF-v2
GitHub: deepguard-deepfake-detection
